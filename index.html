About This Challenge

Welcome to "Are You Smarter Than The Internet?", a uniquely designed IQ test that challenges your conventional thinking. Most online tests are straightforward, but this one is built to subvert your expectations. It's not just about what you know; it's about how you perceive the world around you.

How to Play

Each level presented in this test has a logic of its own. Some require quick reflexes, others require careful reading of the instructions, and a few might even require you to do absolutely nothing. Your goal is to reach Level 20 without failing. If you fail, you start from the beginning—unless you choose to continue with a sponsored retry.

Tips for Success

1. Read carefully: The most obvious answer is often a trap. Look for hidden buttons or double meanings in the text.
2. Be patient: Some levels count down, while others wait for your action. Don't rush unless the timer tells you to.
3. Think outside the box: If a button isn't working, maybe the solution isn't a button at all.
